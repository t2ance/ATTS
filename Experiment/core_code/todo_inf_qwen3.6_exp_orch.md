**Critical correction to vLLM backend doc** (was the load-bearing decision):
the prior comment in `backends/vllm.py:call_sub_model` claimed explorer must
run `enable_thinking=False` to keep strict-JSON closing inside `max_tokens`.
That assumption was retired 2026-05-01: the right knob to grow when thinking-mode
truncates is `max_tokens`, not the `enable_thinking` flag. Forcing thinking-off
in the explorer would silently diverge cache distribution from on-the-fly
orchestrator-generated explores whenever a cache miss occurs, breaking the
like-for-like guarantee that `cache_only` exists to enforce. The explorer and
orchestrator MUST share the same sampling block.

## Anti-patterns to avoid

- Do NOT reuse `_temp` cache dirs (sonnet) -- this arm is about regenerating explores with Qwen.
- Do NOT relaunch serve between benchmarks; one serve, four precaches, four evals.
- Do NOT trust stale `progress.json` from a `_temp` run -- new `log_dir` per benchmark.
- Do NOT downgrade `num_workers` below 128 for precache without a measurement -- under-utilizes the 3-replica serve.
- Do NOT diverge precache-yaml `sampling` from eval-yaml `sampling` -- breaks like-for-like cache distribution invariant.
